## Full Stack Developer

As part of the **development team at Linnea**, I worked as a **Full Stack developer** building and optimizing features for the company's e-commerce platform.

My role focused on **review management**, **automated mailing systems**, **product SEO optimization**, and **database administration**, using a **custom PHP framework** developed in-house.

---

## Context & Approach

The work was carried out within an **agile development environment** with **weekly sprints**, ensuring regular delivery of features and improvements to the e-commerce platform.

Every deployment was accompanied by **detailed documentation** to maintain knowledge sharing and ensure smooth handovers between team members.

---

## Responsibilities

### Review Management System
- Developed a **review sorting and filtering system** to organize customer feedback efficiently  
- Implemented logic to categorize and prioritize reviews based on relevance and sentiment  

### Automated Mailing Operations
- Built **mailing list management** for targeted communication campaigns  
- Automated email responses for specific operations, including **review response workflows**  
- Ensured reliable delivery and tracking of customer communications  

### Product SEO & Visibility Optimization
- Designed and implemented **product sheet evaluation criteria** to improve search engine visibility  
- Defined key attributes and scoring mechanisms to enhance product discoverability on the web  
- Optimized product data structure for better ranking in search results  

### Database Management
- Worked with the **SQL database** to create and modify data as needed  
- Performed data manipulation operations to support feature development and business requirements

### Agile Development & Documentation
- Participated in **weekly sprints** with the development team  
- Produced **detailed documentation** after each deployment to ensure traceability and maintainability

## Tools & Technologies

- **PHP** – custom in-house framework  
- **SQL** – database management and optimization  
- **Agile / Scrum** – weekly sprint cycles  
- **Git** – version control

## Results

- Improved review management workflow and customer engagement
